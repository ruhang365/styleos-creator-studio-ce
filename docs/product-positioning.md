# Product Positioning

Protocol defines the standard.
Creator Studio CE proves the workflow.
Cloud makes it usable for small-B users.
Pro creates the advanced moat.

中文：
Protocol 定标准，CE 跑通流程，Cloud 让小 B 直接用，Pro 建立高级壁垒。

## Relationship

- styleos-protocol: open standard, public schemas, starter rules, synthetic examples.
- Creator Studio CE: runnable community edition product path.
- StyleOS Cloud: planned official hosted service for small-B users.
- StyleOS Pro: planned closed advanced knowledge library.

## Positioning

Creator Studio CE is not the commercial hosted product. It is the open-source product foundation that proves how the StyleOS protocol can become a real workflow.

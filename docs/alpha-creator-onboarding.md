# Alpha Creator Onboarding

StyleOS Creator Studio CE helps creators turn fan consultation into a structured service workflow. The Alpha tests whether this workflow is useful before broader hosted product work.

## Current Alpha Scope

Only the Hairstyle Workflow is active in this Alpha.

The goal is to test:

- service creation
- fan intake
- tag generation
- hairstyle rule matching
- Lite Report generation
- Barber Brief generation
- feedback collection
- Candidate Knowledge extraction

## How To Use It

1. Log in with the Alpha magic link.
2. Open Services.
3. Create a hairstyle service.
4. Open the service detail page.
5. Copy or share the intake link with a synthetic tester.
6. Ask the fan tester to fill the intake form with synthetic or low-risk data.
7. Open Cases.
8. Review the submitted case.
9. Generate tags.
10. Match and select hairstyle rules.
11. Generate the Lite Report.
12. Generate the Barber Brief.
13. Mark the report as delivered.
14. Ask the fan tester to open the report link.
15. Ask the fan tester to submit feedback.
16. Extract Candidate Knowledge after feedback consent.

## What Fans Fill In

Fans provide hairstyle goals, current concerns, face and hair signals, maintenance preferences, constraints, and consent. They should not upload photos or sensitive personal data in this Alpha.

## What Not To Upload

Do not upload or enter:

- real photos
- phone numbers
- WeChat IDs
- ID card numbers
- addresses
- emails
- payment records
- private conversations
- medical or highly sensitive information

## How To Submit Feedback

Use the Alpha feedback form template in [Alpha Feedback Form](../launch/alpha-feedback-form.md). Report product issues with synthetic examples only.

# Alpha Scope Lock

## Alpha Audience

Alpha is limited to 3-5 invited small-B testers.

Target testers:

- hairstyle creators
- outfit and styling creators
- image consultants
- beauty creators
- photography, makeup, and styling studios

## Alpha Data Scope

Alpha testing starts with synthetic data.

Testers must not:

- serve real clients through the Alpha
- upload real photos
- enter sensitive personal information
- enter phone numbers
- enter WeChat IDs
- enter ID card numbers
- enter addresses
- enter private client records

## Alpha Goal

The goal is to validate workflow and perceived value:

- Can creators understand the service flow?
- Can they complete intake-to-report?
- Does Barber Brief feel useful?
- Does feedback help improve knowledge?
- Does Candidate Knowledge represent a meaningful future asset?

## Alpha Non-goal

Alpha is not a public launch, paid launch, client service launch, or broad creator onboarding campaign.

# Non-Goals

The v0.1 product definition does not include:

- App
- mini program
- payment
- full automatic expert replacement
- unauthorized data training
- public real-photo cases
- medical beauty advice
- full image AI analysis
- real expert model
- full Pro library
- official certification
- offline salon integration
- commercial operations admin

## Principle

CE should define and later prove the workflow. It should not claim to be Cloud, Pro, certification, or expert replacement.
